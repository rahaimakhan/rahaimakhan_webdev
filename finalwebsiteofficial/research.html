The Cymatics Connection

The Cymatics Connection refers to the visual and scientific study of how sound frequencies physically shape matter. Derived from the Greek word kyma, meaning “wave,” cymatics reveals the hidden geometry of sound by using materials like sand, water, or powders on vibrating surfaces. When specific tones are played, the particles organize into intricate patterns that become more complex with higher frequencies—demonstrating that sound has the power to structure physical reality.
